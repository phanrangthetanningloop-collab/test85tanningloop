2N1Đ — Coast & Farm

$65 /person
Self riding · ~160km · Max 6

Landscape

▾
Day 1: Coastal North + Mui Dinh (~80km)▾

Be ready at 8:00 AM at lodge Tan An. Breakfast before departure. Set off at 9:00 AM heading north — Vietnam's driest, most unspoiled coastline.

Route
Lodge Tan An → Dong Hai seafood market → Bai Thung ancient coral beach → Mui Dinh Lighthouse (1905) → Son Hai wild beach → Đam Vua salt fields (golden hour) → Lodge
🐟 Dong Hai Market🏖 Bai Thung Beach🗼 Mui Dinh 1905🌊 Son Hai Beach🧂 Salt Fields
Day 2: Farm + Inland (~80km)▾

Breakfast at 8:00 AM. Morning at the goat farm — same farm supplying Huynh De hotpot in HCMC. Then inland through canyons and wind farms. Return ~4pm.

Route
Lodge → Huynh De Goat Farm → Bimong Po Rome Cham Tower → Phuoc Khang spring → Stone Park → Đam Nai lagoon kayak → Return ~4:00 PM
🐐 Goat Farm🏛 Bimong Po Rome💧 Phuoc Khang🪨 Stone Park🛶 Kayak

Landscape

Same as 2N1Đ Day 1. Coast north — Dong Hai, Bai Thung, Mui Dinh lighthouse, Son Hai, salt fields at golden hour.

Depart 7:30am. Road climbs from sea level to 1,200m in 1.5h — temperature drops 10°C entering the canopy. Local Raglai park guide joins inside.

Route
Lodge → VQG Phuoc Binh → Chapor Waterfall → Gia Nhong river → Raglai village (cơm lam + black pork) → Return by 2:30pm
🌊 Chapor Waterfall🏘 Raglai Village🍚 Cơm lam + black pork

4N3Đ — Phuoc Binh Wild Loop

$120 /person
Self riding · ~270km · Max 6

Landscape

▾
Day 1: Coastal North + Mui Dinh (~80km)▾

Same as all packages — coast north, Dong Hai, Bai Thung, Mui Dinh lighthouse, Son Hai, salt fields.

Route
Lodge → Dong Hai → Bai Thung → Mui Dinh → Son Hai → Salt Fields → Lodge
Day 2: Farm + Inland (~80km)▾

Same as all packages — goat farm, Cham tower, spring, Stone Park, lagoon kayak.

Route
Lodge → Goat Farm → Bimong Po Rome → Phuoc Khang → Stone Park → Kayak → Lodge
Day 3: Deep into Phuoc Binh + Overnight (~70km)▾

Depart 7:30am. Unlike the day trip version, today you stay overnight inside the park at a Raglai stilt house (100k/person). No phone signal 18–24h. Dinner: cơm lam + black pork + jungle banana wine around the fire.

Route
Lodge → VQG Phuoc Binh → Chapor Waterfall → Gia Nhong river → Raglai village → Overnight stilt house
🌊 Chapor Waterfall🏘 Raglai Village🏕 Stilt House Overnight🍖 Black pork + jungle wine
Day 4: Cloud Hunt + Wild Gaur (~40km + depart)▾

5:30am — wake to mist and jungle sounds. Head out quietly to the wild gaur sanctuary — largest herd in Vietnam. Back to village for breakfast, then ChuRu village. Depart 11am, base by 1pm — time for afternoon bus back to HCMC.

⚠️ No phone signal D3 night → D4 morning (18–24h). Someone at base monitors your messages.
Route
Stilt house → Wild gaur sanctuary (5:30am) → Breakfast → ChuRu village → Depart 11:00 → Lodge by 13:00
☁️ 5:30am cloud hunt🦬 Wild gaur herd🏘 ChuRu Village
